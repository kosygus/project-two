Who is to blame?

Fast fashion can be defined as cheap, trendy
clothing, that samples ideas from the catwalk or
celebrity culture and turns them into garments in
high street stores at breakneck speed to meet
consumer demand.

The idea is to get the newest
styles on the market as fast as possible, so
shoppers can snap them up while they are still at
the height of their popularity, and then discard
them after a few wears.
