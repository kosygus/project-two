FAQS

What should I do with my old clothes?
Donate any clothes you no longer wear!
Never just throw them away. Whether it is
to a local homeless shelter, or just the Goodwill
- donate, donate, donate.

Where should I buy my clothes?
Purchase clothing from sustainable brands. Sustainable
brands do tend to run a bit expensive, however. If you find
yourself unable to buy from sustainable brands - go thrift shopping!
Second hand stores not only help the planet out,
but they also help out your wallet. It is a win-win situation,
and you never know what you might find!
